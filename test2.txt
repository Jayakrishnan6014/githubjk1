Marvel movies are based on characters from Marvel Comics, like Iron Man, Captain America, and Spider-Man.

They are produced mainly by Marvel Studios and are part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

The MCU began in 2008 with Iron Man, which introduced Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark.

These movies are known for action, humor, and strong storytelling.

They connect through shared storylines, leading to big crossover films like Avengers: Endgame.

Marvel movies often explore themes of heroism, responsibility, and teamwork.

Popular heroes include Thor, Hulk, Black Panther, Doctor Strange, and Captain Marvel.

Villains like Thanos and Loki play major roles in shaping the stories.

Marvel films are famous worldwide and have huge fan followings.

They inspire not only entertainment but also pop culture, merchandise, and fan communities.
